Co-founded with [maryaleks.dev](https://maryaleks.dev). ZooMinder helps pet owners manage their animals' healthcare by scheduling medication reminders and tracking treatment histories.

Features smart reminders for daily pills, monthly flea and tick prevention, weekly supplements, post-surgery medications, insulin injections, and more. Supports flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, or custom), automatic dose logging, multi-pet management, and encrypted cloud synchronization across devices.

Available on the Google Play Store with a free tier (one pet, one medication, 7-day history) and a premium plan for unlimited pets and complete history.
