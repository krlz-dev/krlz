As a Senior Developer at Portavita LLC (2018-2024), I contributed to the Portavita DVZA Services platform — a critical data services provider for healthcare institutions in the Netherlands. Operational from 2020 to 2024, this platform processed millions of patient records monthly.

Key contributions included developing multiple microservices within the DVZA platform: data validation, metrics generation, data processing, enrichment, and delivery through FHIR-compliant REST services. The backend was built with Scala and Akka, with Kafka and Kafka Streams handling messaging, and PostgreSQL (with Citus for distributed queries) powering the data layer.

I also led the Patient Assistant service, supporting patients receiving anticoagulation treatment across Dutch healthcare institutions — splitting it into three microservices responsible for data processing, enrichment, and delivery.
