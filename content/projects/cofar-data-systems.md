As a Data Systems Analyst at Laboratorios COFAR (2016-2017) in La Paz, Bolivia, I worked on improving existing system modules for the pharmaceutical company during a critical period of architectural migration.

The company was transitioning many of its modules from a monolithic architecture to microservices. I contributed to this migration effort, analyzing existing data flows and improving modules to align with the new distributed architecture.
